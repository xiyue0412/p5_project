# Reflection

### What prompting techniques did you use?

I started with a simple prompt and added more details step by step. I changed the lighting, furniture, materials and atmosphere to make the image closer to my idea.

### Was there anything that you struggled to communicate?

It was difficult to get the exact layout I wanted. Sometimes the AI changed the furniture or added objects that I did not ask for.

### How much of the final result was your own making?

I created the idea and improved the prompts after each image. The AI generated the images, but I decided the style and the final design.
